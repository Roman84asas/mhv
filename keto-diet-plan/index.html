Keto diet plan for women and men

01

We need additional information about you

Your BMI (the indicator of a person’s weight status), gender and age are not enough to create the perfect keto menu plan. ...
To better understand your body's needs, our nutritionists diagnose you and make careful dietary adjustments to meet your health needs. Please answer each question in our quiz as honestly as possible, it won't take long.

02

Your health affects your weigh loss

Being obese or overweight increases the risk of many health problems. However, your health state can affect how quickly or slowly you lose weight, as well. We will analyze your answers and offer an individual solution, depending on the indicators and health status:...
  • Functioning of the heart and other internal organs.
  • High blood pressure.
  • Condition of hair, skin and nails.
  • Sexual function and libido.
  • Psycho-emotional stress.
  • High levels of cholesterol and/or blood sugar.

03

Can I do keto just for a month?

Does it make sense for me to go on keto for just 1 month? Our nutritionists get this question a lot. ...
Our opinion is that if you are really close to your target weight, you can probably reach your goal in a month or two. But if you have a lot of extra pounds, a 1 month keto diet plan may be partially effective. Set realistic goals and then you can really achieve them.
Yes, you can lose 4-5 kilograms in the first month, but it is very likely that this result will not persist for a long time. We will develop nutrition plans especially for you, and these plans will help you not only lose weight, but also make your body and lifestyle healthy.
Don't aim for quick results. Strive for results that will be with you for years to come.

04

What is the healthy version of keto diet?

The healthy keto plan is a weight loss program that should be compiled by a professional nutritionist. If you are not an expert in weight loss, seek help from people who ...
understand this issue. Advice from our nutritionists is personalized to suit your needs. Our years of experience in healthy eating ensures that your body receives adequate amounts of nutrients, combined with a sustainable weight loss.

05

How much weight do you lose on keto in 3 months?

By following all the rules and reducing the number of calories by 15-20%, on a keto diet, you can lose 12-15 kilograms in 3 months. Obviously, people gain weight at different rates, and it is understandable that the rate of weight loss will also vary. ...
The number of kilograms you lose depends on the following factors:
  • Calories in your keto food plan.
  • Strict adherence to nutritionist’s recommendations.
  • Initial body weight.
  • The level of physical activity.
  • Your state of health.
  • Features and metabolic rate.
  • Gender and age.

06

Keto on a budget meal plan

Easy cheap keto meal plan with lots of fats and proteins will help you lose weight without having to spend a lot of money. ...
Ideas and strategies to reduce costs:
  • Limit eating out.
  • Make shopping lists.
  • Check the expiration date so you don't throw away food.
  • Buy in bulk when it makes sense.
  • Visit sales, follow promotions in the store.
  • Choose cheaper protein products more often.
