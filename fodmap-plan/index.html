What can you eat on a FODMAP diet

If you eat foods that are said to be good for health and weight loss but your gut doesn't respond well to them, how about trying a low FODMAP diet?Here is an easy way to understand that you are on your way to your goal.You can consume these products, as they do not contain FODMAPs:

  • Shellfish, sea fish (fresh and frozen).
  • Beef, lamb, poultry, pork (including bacon and ham).
  • Vegetarian protein products.
  • Eggs.
  • Soya mince.
  • Tempeh and firm tofu.

List of foods to choose or prepare correctly

Avoid these products

  • Milk
  • Bread, biscuits and rolls made from wheat, rye, barley or amaranth
  • Breakfast cereals based on ground or bran wheat, rye or barley

Foods you can eat

  • Lactose-free milk
  • Bread, biscuits and rolls made from rice, corn, buckwheat, oatmeal, potato or tapioca flour
  • Breakfast cereals made from oat, rice, corn bran or flakes

Be careful with nutritional balance

Low FODMAP foods can be very nutritious. Many of them contain the necessary nutrients for our body, combined with a large number of calories. Calories are not our enemies, they are a source of energy for daily activities. But in order to lose weight, you need to reduce the number of calories by about 15-20%.

The same products may contain different amounts of FODMAP depending on the cooking method and the place of purchase

Yes, this is true and therefore you need to think about how the products are prepared. FODMAPs have not been found in chicken. If you want to buy fried chicken at a fast food restaurant, it's best not to do so without looking into the situation. You can do this if the products package says "NO FODMAP".

Self-following a low-FODMAP diet can lead to nutrient deficiency

If you want to do everything right and succeed, you need to consult a doctor, a nutritionist or order a professional low FODMAP meal plan. Deficiency of fiber, vitamins and minerals has a negative impact on the weight loss program, as well as on the functioning of the gastrointestinal tract.

Eat at fixed times

Developing the right habits is the best way to achieve good health, a more fulfilling life and, of course, the desired figure. Follow the regimen and eat 3 times a day at about the same time. Never skip meals. Fasting and a very strict diet can lead to anxiety, stress, and even depression.

Can your health affect your weight loss?

Being obese or overweight increases the risk of many health problems. However, your health state can affect how quickly or slowly you lose weight, as well. We will analyze your answers and offer an individual solution, depending on the indicators and health status:...
  • Bowel problems.
  • High blood pressure.
  • Sexual function and libido.
  • High levels of cholesterol and/or blood sugar.
  • Functioning of the heart and other internal organs.
  • Condition of hair, skin and nails.
  • Psycho-emotional stress.

03

Can you do FODMAP diet without dietician?

We will not argue that it is impossible to lose weight without the help of a nutritionist and an individual nutrition plan. Some people can do this, especially if ...
they have just a few extra kilos. However, unless you are a dietician or nutritionist, it may be difficult for you to distinguish a good diet with long-term results from another. The only truly effective way to lose weight is a well-thought-out diet, and it is desirable that it be compiled by an experienced nutritionist. Try to always consult with a specialist before going on a diet. The treatment of any health problem must be linked to a correct diagnosis by expert opinion. Making your own diet choices you've heard or read about can do more harm than good.

04

Can you lose weight on a low FODMAP diet?

The short answer is yes. The MHW Low FODMAP meal plan is not only a nutritional model that improves bowel function. Depending on your initial weight and ...
your other indicators, we will develop an individual low-calorie meal plan. The most effective weight loss principle is to create a negative calorie balance, and we will help you achieve this. 

05

How long does it take for my IBS symptoms to disappear when following a low FODMAP diet?

Studies show that IBS symptoms improve in 3 out of 4 people who follow a healthy eating pattern. The time to achieve positive results is ...
individual for each person.
Many people notice an improvement in their IBS symptoms before they complete the 30 day low FODMAP meal plan. Others may take up to 6 weeks or more to identify and treat the cause of IBS.
