Our Meal Plans are based on LCHF diet, which is considered the most effective and adequate approach to a healthy lifestyle. While ketogenic diets have proven to be deteriorating for your health in the long run, we’ve decided to adopt LCHF as the best option out there for people suffering from diabetes, high cholesterol, chronic fatigue, etc.. However, we don’t just take the principles of LCHF diet and make up a Meal Plan.

Why a LCHF meal plan is your go-to option

Most weight loss plans

  • are made by one-size-fits-all principle, without regard to your health
  • provide a fast result, which doesn’t last for a long time
  • the yo-yo dieting harms your overall health and physical appearance
  • are not intended to become your lifestyle and change your life quality for good
  • are not adjusted based on your changes and progress

Our customized plans

  • are developed, taking into account your health issues and bodily needs
  • focus on solving your weight loss issue while improving your health
  • help your body develop a habit of eating properly
  • come with regular check-ups for a thorough reassessment and further adjustment
  • don’t imply searching for foods that are hard to find

Our approach to customized LCHF plans builds a strong momentum for your perfect weight loss journey.
